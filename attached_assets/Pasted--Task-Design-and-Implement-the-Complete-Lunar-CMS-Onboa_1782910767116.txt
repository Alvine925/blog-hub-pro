# Task: Design and Implement the Complete Lunar CMS Onboarding Experience

## Objective

Design a world-class onboarding flow that takes a user from signup to publishing their first blog post in under 10 minutes.

The onboarding should feel intelligent, personalized, and guided.

Unlike traditional CMS platforms, users should not be required to manually configure collections, APIs, or technical settings before they can create content.

The system should understand the user's website, automatically create the workspace context, and use AI to help them generate better content.

The onboarding experience should feel similar to a combination of:

* Notion
* Linear
* Canva
* Shopify
* HubSpot

Simple, guided, and highly personalized.

---

# Core Principle

The user should never see a blank dashboard.

The system should understand:

* Who they are
* What website they own
* What industry they operate in
* Who their competitors are
* What content opportunities exist

before they create their first piece of content.

# Final Goal

The onboarding experience should feel like hiring a content strategist rather than setting up a CMS. By the time users enter the dashboard, the platform should already understand their business, website, competitors, audience, and content opportunities. The user should immediately see value and be able to publish their first blog post with minimal effort while the AI handles the research, context gathering, and content recommendations automatically.
